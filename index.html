Service 1 of 5 · Lead Gen

Google Ads

What it is
We get their company ranked at the top of Google and bring in about 10 extra details a week. No money up front, fully performance based.
What to tell the client
"We put you at the #1 spot on Google. You only pay for qualified leads, real buying-intent calls, and it's just 10% of your basic detail."
Price
10% of their interior/exterior detail per qualified lead. A $250 detail = $25 per lead, flat no matter the job size.
Your commission
Setter 40%, Closer 25% of the first bi-weekly invoice. No setter on the deal? Closer gets the full 40%.
Talk track

The magic line: even if that lead books a $1,000 ceramic, we still only charge $25. Qualified means buying potential, never price-shoppers or booked-out slots.

Service 2 of 5 · Web

Website Design

What it is
Premium sites built to convert AND get found, on Google (SEO) and on AI like ChatGPT (AEO). Mobile-first, booking + payments, live in 7 days.
What to tell the client
"There's a big difference between looking good and performing good. Yours will book jobs and show up on Google and ChatGPT. One extra job pays for it."
Price
$500 basic (looks good) or $1,000 to $1,500 optimized, plus a $50/mo retainer. Anchor at $2,000, financing available, or bundle it into DGS.
Your commission
Ad Manager 30%, Setter 20%, Closer 30% from Setter + 20% from Ad Manager.
Talk track

The full teardown play (phone test, show one of ours, do the math) lives in the Website Pitch deck.

Service 3 of 5 · AI

AI Receptionist

What it is
Answers every call and text and books straight into their calendar. Or backup mode: it only catches the calls they miss. Fully customizable, can even clone their voice.
What to tell the client
"You'll never miss a lead again. It answers, handles questions, and books jobs while you're under a car or asleep. You get your nights and weekends back."
Price
Setup $1,000 (backup mode) up to $1,500 (full implementation), then a $500/month residual.
Your commission
Setter or Ad Manager 25%, Closer 40% of the first bi-weekly invoice.
Talk track

For anti-AI clients, sell backup mode first (only answers missed calls), then demo it live. Hearing it work flips the skeptics.

Service 4 of 5 · Software

Detailer Growth System (DGS)

What it is
Our own software. Tracks every lead in one place, sends mass text and email campaigns to past and no-show clients, and fires automatic review requests and reminder texts to kill no-shows. Booking page, upsells, memberships CRM.
What to tell the client
"One place for every lead. Text your whole customer list a promo in one click, automate your reviews, and stop no-shows. Your past customers become repeat revenue."
Price
$500 to $750 per month, billed monthly. We provide the prebuilt campaigns they send.
Your commission
Setter or Ad Manager 25%, Closer 60% of the first month.
Talk track

Never call it GoHighLevel or white-label. It is our software that we built. DGS is the anchor you bundle the website and AI into.

Service 5 of 5 · Meta

Meta (Facebook) Ads

What it is
Brings in about 3 to 4 extra ceramic coatings a week. We build, write, and post the ads and the entire campaign. The client supplies nothing.
What to tell the client
"This is where your $1,000 ceramic jobs come from. We build and run all of it. You only pay when we actually book you a job."
Price
20% per booked ceramic job. Client covers ad spend (we recommend ~$35/day). $1,000 one-time setup. Payment plans available.
Your commission
Performance model. Follows the Standard Lead Gen split (Setter 40% / Closer 25% of first invoice). Confirm with Grant.
Talk track

"We only get paid when you get paid" is the killer line for a burned client. Lead with it.

Part 2 · Payment Collection

How we collect, in order

1
Invoice
Bi-weekly, monthly, or one-time. First payment is collected before onboarding (except Lead Gen). Recurring is set up via Square after the first payment.
2
Contract + payment
Agreement is signed before the invoice is sent. Payment link goes out right after signature. Onboarding call is scheduled only after payment confirms.
3
Recurring billing
Auto-draft every 30 days. 3-day grace, paused after 3 days, late fee after 7, account terminated after 14.
Talk track

Order is everything: sign, then pay, then onboard. Never schedule onboarding before the payment clears.
